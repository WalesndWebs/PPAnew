Chigozirim Irunokhai

Group Managing Director

An Impact-Driven Investment Professional: Leading Sustainable Growth and Portfolio Management

A dynamic investment professional with extensive experience in managing portfolios, developing financial strategies, and executing impactful investment projects, with a strong focus on ESG integration and sustainable sectors. Holds an MBA in Financial Management from Lagos State University and a B.Sc. in Microbiology from the University of Ibadan. She is also a Sponsored Individual (Fund Manager) certified by the Securities & Exchange Commission, and a member of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers and The Association of Investment Advisers and Portfolio Managers.

As the founding Managing Director/CEO of Sky Capital Asset Management Ltd, has been instrumental in leading strategic direction, overseeing investments, and establishing trusted relationships with shareholders and regulatory bodies. Prior to this, as Head of Portfolio Management, has managed multi-asset portfolios, directed policy formulation, and executed investment strategies to align with client objectives.

Career highlights include successfully growing global investment portfolios from N35 billion to over N60 billion at Veritas Pensions Ltd, achieving 100% targeted returns on fixed-income portfolios, and designing innovative financial products like Dollar Mutual Funds and Treasury Bill Notes. With expertise in financial modeling, due diligence, valuation, and a deep understanding of the African investment landscape, She is adept at driving value creation and fostering sustainable development through strategic investment and active ownership.

Raphael Famuyiwa

Group Head, Investment Portfolio Manager

Leadership Overview

Raphael Famuyiwa is a dynamic finance leader with over eight years of experience in portfolio management, derivatives trading, and quantitative research. As the founder and Portfolio Manager of Ocean-Ways Partners (2023–August 2025), he built a successful investment firm, leading a team to outperform benchmark indices across major markets in 2023 and achieve a 20% ROI through strategic restructuring. Since August 2025, he has served as Group Head of Finance at Prodigy Investment, where he oversees financial strategy, drives advanced analytics initiatives, and enhances group-wide portfolio performance.

Innovation and Expertise: A pioneer in financial technology, Raphael independently designed and developed "Mcmarket," a cutting-edge, pre-launch software for options and derivatives trading, completed in August 2025. This functional platform integrates Monte Carlo, Black-Scholes, and Binomial models with real-time API data, streamlining strategy execution and improving efficiency by an estimated 20%.

Education: Raphael holds an ACCA/MBA qualification earned through the integrated program at the London School of Business and Finance (LSBF, 2013–2016), complemented by a Bachelor's Degree in Marketing from Madonna University, Okija, Nigeria (2008–2011). He is currently pursuing CFA Level 1 (planned for 2026).
