We are your trusted partner on the journey to financial excellence.

Prodigy Group Limited is a diversified financial services holding company focused on providing progressive financial solutions across Nigeria and beyond. Our subsidiaries include; Prodigy Finance, Prodigy Portfolio Advisers, and Prodigy Brokercom.

Maintaining our reputation as providers of dependable and innovative financial services in the region is important to us. To this end, we are continually deepening our business lines and expanding what we offer through our subsidiaries.

The Group operates a shared service framework in which some business support functions are shared across the group in line with regulatory requirements. These business support functions include internal audit, internal control, compliance, information technology, risk management, and administrative services. The costs incurred are shared in an agreed ratio that reflects the rate of consumption by each entity. At the core of the shared service model is cost optimization and process efficiency.
