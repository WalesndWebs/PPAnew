About Us

Making economic prosperity more accessible to everyone

Prodigy Group is a diversified financial services conglomerate and a leading asset management and financial advisory firm in Nigeria. We started our journey in 2010 with a vision to democratize access to sophisticated financial services, and have since evolved into a comprehensive financial solutions provider through our three specialized subsidiaries.

We are a team of experienced fund managers and financial professionals with a breadth of strong-performing products across multiple asset classes, easily accessible to individuals and institutions. Our subsidiaries - Prodigy Finance, Prodigy Portfolio Advisers, and Prodigy Brokercom - work in synergy to provide comprehensive financial solutions.

We are registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission and are versatile in financial transactions and investment strategies that meet the needs of investors in an emerging economy like Nigeria. Our strategies are dedicated to preserving investors' wealth while maximizing the value that they receive.

We are positioned to build wealth for clients across all economic classes and are the trusted partner in growing your wealth through innovative financial products, strategic lending solutions, and commodities trading expertise.
