ABOUT US

Making economic prosperity more accessible to everyone

Prodigy Group is a diversified financial services conglomerate and a leading asset management and financial advisory firm in Nigeria. Initially, we started as a single investment advisory service in 2010, and in 2015 morphed into Prodigy Group, a fully-fledged, diversified financial services company.

We are a team of experienced fund managers with a breadth of strong-performing products across multiple asset classes, easily accessible to individuals and institutions.

We are registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission as a Portfolio Manager and a Corporate Investment Adviser and are versatile in financial transactions and investment strategies that meet the needs of investors in an emerging economy like Nigeria.

Our strategies are dedicated to preserving investors' wealth while maximizing the value that they receive. We are positioned to build wealth for clients across all economic classes and are the trusted partner in growing your wealth.
