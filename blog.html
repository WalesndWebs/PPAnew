Our Story: Building Financial Bridges

At Prodigy Group, we believe that financial constraints should never stand in the way of your goals. Whether you're a student with a project to complete, an entrepreneur needing working capital, or a salary earner facing an unexpected expense, we are here to bridge that gap with dignity, speed, and transparency.

Founded on the principle that financial services should be accessible to all, Prodigy Group has emerged as a trusted partner in Nigeria's financial landscape. Our diversified approach encompasses three core subsidiaries: Prodigy Finance, Prodigy Portfolio Advisers, and Prodigy Brokercom, each dedicated to serving different aspects of your financial journey.

What We Stand For

Unwavering Trust

Trust is the foundation of every relationship. We build lasting partnerships through transparency, reliability, and consistent delivery on our promises.

Impenetrable Security

Your financial security is our top priority. We employ cutting-edge technology and robust processes to protect your data and investments.

Radical Transparency

No hidden fees, no complex jargon. We believe in clear communication and honest dealings in every transaction and interaction.

Innovation in Action: Prodigy Flexi Loan

Our commitment to innovation is exemplified in our flagship product, the Prodigy Flexi Loan. This micro-lending solution embodies everything we stand for:

Simple & Accessible

Our process is simple and straightforward. Fill out a short application on our secure website or mobile app, and get quick approval through our data-driven model.

No Collateral Required

We don't require any physical assets to secure your loan. We trust in your ability to repay based on transparent terms and your financial profile.

Fast Disbursement

Get the funds you need directly in your account in a timely manner. No hidden fees or charges - what you see is what you get.

Built for Everyone

Whether you're a student, salary earner, or micro-business owner, our Flexi-Loan is more than just a loan - it's a partnership to help you achieve your aspirations.

Our Commitment to Nigeria

Prodigy Group is proudly Nigerian, built by Nigerians for Nigerians. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities in our market, and we're committed to developing solutions that address real needs in our communities.

Our approach goes beyond traditional banking. We leverage technology to create seamless, digital-first experiences while maintaining the personal touch that builds lasting relationships. From Lagos to Abuja, from Port Harcourt to Kano, we're working to make financial services more accessible to every Nigerian.
